This system is a complete supportive tool for vast community of farmers to help them improving their lifestyle. Through this system there will be a direct connection between government and farmers belonging to different regions of our country. Through this system the farmers will be able to sell their crops at the best price to the government. First farmer will login through its unique id and will upload its crop quantity and estimated price. After that agriculture inspector will come to farmer field to check the quality of crop and will give the ratings on a scale of 0 to 10(least to most) and according to the rating price estimation will be decided. Once crop is sold it will be sent to respective warehouse and from there will be sent to different regions of a country by government where it is needed. If the productivity is more than required then government will connect to international market for the export of these crops and can make good connection with the global world.
